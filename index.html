My story

My artistic odyssey began at the Government College of Fine Arts in Chennai, where I honed my skills in metal design and painting. This journey of self-discovery has fueled my desire to translate emotions and concepts into captivating visual art.

For over four decades, I've had the privilege of shaping young artistic minds. As a lecturer and head of various art departments at the Government College of Fine Arts in Chennai, I've been a part of a dynamic educational journey. Guiding aspiring artists and witnessing their growth has been a fulfilling part of my artistic endeavors.
